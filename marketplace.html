PRO TIPS

🔍
Search Smart
Use specific terms like "TKL mechanical keyboard" or "Gateron Yellow switches" for better results. Generic searches surface too much noise.
⭐
Check Seller Ratings
Always check seller ratings and reviews. Buy from official brand storefronts on Amazon/Flipkart for authenticity guarantees.
💰
Wait for Sales
Big Billion Days (Flipkart) and Great Indian Festival (Amazon) offer significant peripheral discounts. Plan big purchases around these events.
